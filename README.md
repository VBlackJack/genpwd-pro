## Generation modes

GenPwd Pro offers **3 different ways** to create passwords. Choose according to your needs.

### Mode 1: Pronounceable (Syllables)

**What is it?**
Creates passwords that look like real words, easy to read and pronounce.

**When to use it?**
- When you need to type the password often
- For a password you want to memorize
- When a site refuses special characters

**Settings:**
- **Length**: from 12 to 25 characters (16 recommended)
- **Digits**: add 1 to 3 digits
- **Special characters**: add symbols like `!@#$%`

### Mode 2: Word phrase (Passphrase)

**What is it?**
Combines several real words from the dictionary, separated by dashes or spaces.

**When to use it?**
- For very important accounts (bank, primary email)

- **Case**: Capitals at word start, ALL CAPS, or all lowercase

**Tip:** Create a mental story with the words to remember them more easily.

**What is it?**
Transforms a word or phrase you know by replacing certain letters with numbers or symbols.

**Example:**
`P@55W0RD!` (from "PASSWORD")

- `a` ‚Üí `@`

**When to use it?**
- To transform a personal phrase into a safe password

- To add complexity to a simple word

## Privacy and security

### Your privacy is our priority

**GenPwd Pro collects NO data.** That's a promise.

- ‚ùå Your passwords are **never sent over the Internet**
- ‚ùå No user account, no registration
- ‚ùå No tracking cookies
- ‚ùå No advertising

### How is it secure?

**1. Truly random generation**
GenPwd Pro uses your device's random number generator (the same one banks use), not fake predictable combinations.

**2. Maximum complexity**
Generated passwords would withstand thousands of years of brute-force attack, even with a supercomputer.

**3. Open Source code**
Anyone can verify that the code doesn't do anything malicious. The project is public on [GitHub](https://github.com/VBlackJack/genpwd-pro).

**4. Local storage only**
If you use history or saved configurations, everything stays on your device. Uninstall the app, and everything disappears.

**5. Optional cloud sync**
If you enable synchronization (Android only), your data is **encrypted before sending**. Even if someone intercepts your vault, they can't decrypt it without your master password.

### Security tips for your passwords

**‚úÖ DO:**
- Use a **different password for each site**
- Choose **at least 16 characters** for important accounts
- Enable **two-factor authentication** (2FA) when possible
- Keep your passwords in a **password manager**
- Change your passwords **every 6-12 months** or **immediately after a data breach**

**‚ùå DON'T:**
- Reuse the same password everywhere
- Use personal information (name, birth date, dog's name...)
- Write your passwords on visible Post-its
- Share your passwords via SMS or email
- Use dictionary words without transformation

### What if I forget my password?

**GenPwd Pro doesn't store your passwords** (unless you enable history). We recommend:

1. **Use a password manager** like Bitwarden (free) or 1Password
2. **Enable history** in GenPwd Pro (stored locally on your device)
3. **Export your passwords** and store them in a safe place (encrypted vault, encrypted USB key)
4. **Enable recovery options** on your accounts (backup email, phone number)
